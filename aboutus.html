Who We Are

About Us

Stork Drop is an logistics trucking app, that is here to solve problems like the increase in blue-collar labor shortage and the constant population shift that has mounted pressure on the freight industry. We are going to solve these issues by automating the logistics industry using our trucking app that will connect the requestors directly to credible carriers for fast and easy trucking. Also, we give our users the privilege of tracking their goods in real-time, thus saving time and having the peace of mind to go about other daily activities.
Stork Drop is here to brigde the gap between technology and logistics. Founded by Elorm Axolu,a tech entrepreneur in Ghana, as a startup in 2018,and is focused on solving more problems through technology. Stork Drop offers Carriers and Carrier-requestors the connection to grow their businesses via our web and mobile applications. Carrier-requestors also able to track their goods in real-time.
